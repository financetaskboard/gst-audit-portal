GSTR-1A — Upload Amendment PDF / GST Portal PDF Auto-Extract
📝 What is GSTR-1A? GSTR-1A is the amendment return to correct errors in an already-submitted GSTR-1. The GST Portal only provides a PDF download (no JSON). Upload the PDF here — the portal will automatically extract all amendment figures (B2BA, B2CSA, B2CLA, EXPA, CDNRA) using AI-assisted parsing.

📥 How to get the PDF: gst.gov.in → Returns → GSTR-1A → Select Month & Year → View / Download PDF → Upload here.
📄 Upload GSTR-1A Amendment PDF
Upload one PDF per company per month. Period & GSTIN are auto-detected from the PDF — just select the company and click Import.
